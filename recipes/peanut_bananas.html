Peanut Butter Bananas

Description

This is one of my favortite snacks from when I was a kid. All you need is peanut butter and bananas. Its very simple and requires little ingredients so it should make the perfect breakfast side or midday snack.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Fresh Bananas
  • Knife

Steps

  1. Grab your banana and make a cut down the middle with the peel intact
  2. Pick up peanut butter with knife
  3. Smear the peanut butter inside the slit made previously
  4. Peel the banana and enjoy!
